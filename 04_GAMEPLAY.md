# Gameplay Mechanics

## Nekomancer NFTs
- Each NFT is part of the **888 Genesis collection**.
- Unique traits, rarity, and personality.
- Essential for quests, habitats, and breeding.

## Quests
- Embark on quests to earn $PAW tokens and NFTs.
- Quest types: Simple, Challenge, Rare Reward.

## Habitats
- Passive income via $PAW tokens.
- Fully customizable and upgradable.
- Can house multiple Nekomancers to increase rewards.

## $PAW Token
- In-game purchases, breeding fees, governance participation.
- Earned from quests and habitat rewards.

## Breeding Guide
- Combine traits from two Nekomancers to create a new unique NFT.
- Trait inheritance depends on parent NFTs.
