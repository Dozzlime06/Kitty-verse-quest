# Getting Started

1. **Set Up a Wallet**  
   - Recommended: MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet.

2. **Bridge Assets to Base**  
   - Transfer ETH or other supported tokens to Base.

3. **Mint Your First Nekomancer NFT**  
   - Connect your wallet to the official Base launch page.  
   - Genesis NFT collection supply: **1111 unique Nekomancers**.  
   - Each NFT has unique traits and can participate in quests, habitats, and breeding.
