# Introduction to Nekomancy

**What is Nekomancy?**  
A Play-to-Earn adventure game on Base featuring collectible cat NFTs (**Nekomancer NFTs**), quests, habitats, and social gameplay.

**Genesis NFT Collection:**  
- 4444 unique **Nekomancer NFTs**
- Each Nekomancer has unique traits and personality
- Essential for quests, habitats, and breeding

**Vision & Mission**  
Bring cat art to life in a fully interactive P2E universe, blending collectible NFTs, social experiences, and rewarding gameplay.

**Why Base Network?**  
- Low transaction fees  
- Fast confirmation times  
- Coinbase ecosystem integration

**Meet the Nekomancers**  
Your cat art comes alive as main characters for adventures, quests, and breeding in Nekomancy!
